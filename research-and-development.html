Babu One

COVID-19 Treatment

Babu-One™ is a novel drug developed against SARS-CoV2, and its de novo design is based on the highly adaptable, infinitely reproducible, and non-immunogenic aptamers: short and highly structured oligonucleotides. With our modified process of design, the selective aptamers are solubilized to be sprayed right into the nasopharyngeal airway.

HOW ?

Covering your airways like a shield protects its user, Babu-One™ particles will protect your airway cells from the attack of the virus and prevent the virus from wreaking more havoc on those who have already contracted it. Thus, Babu-One™ will both protect those who need protection and treat those who need treatment.
